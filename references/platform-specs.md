# Platform specs & safe zones (Meta + TikTok)

Read the section that matches the detected aspect ratio (from `probe.sh`). Use
these to score "Mobile fit & safe zones" and to sanity-check whether the creative
matches its likely placement.

## Aspect ratios → placements
| Ratio | Resolution (typical) | Placement |
|---|---|---|
| 9:16 (≈0.56) | 1080×1920 | Reels, Stories, TikTok, Shorts |
| 4:5 (0.80) | 1080×1350 | Meta feed (highest-real-estate feed ratio) |
| 1:1 (1.00) | 1080×1080 | Feed (older, less screen space) |
| 16:9 (1.78) | 1920×1080 | YouTube, landscape feed |

For paid social DTC, **9:16 and 4:5 are the workhorses.** A 16:9 or 1:1 video
running as a Reel/TikTok wastes screen space and usually underperforms — flag it.

## TikTok safe zone (9:16)
The right side and bottom are covered by UI, so keep text/logos/CTA clear of:
- **Right rail (icons):** ~140px on the right is covered by the profile/like/
  comment/share/sound icons.
- **Bottom:** ~120px caption + the account handle and (for ads) the CTA button
  sit across the lower portion — keep critical text out of roughly the bottom
  ~20% of the frame.
- **Top:** keep ~10% clear of the "Following / For You" tabs.
- TikTok is **sound-on by default** and UGC-native — voiceover and an authentic,
  non-polished feel help; overproduced "TV commercial" creative tends to feel
  like an ad and gets skipped.

## Meta Reels / Stories safe zone (9:16)
- Keep key text and the CTA out of roughly the **top ~14%** and **bottom ~20%**
  of the frame — that's where the profile, caption, and CTA button render.
- Center the message in the middle ~66% vertical band.

## Meta feed (4:5 / 1:1)
- Less UI overlap than Reels; most of the frame is usable.
- **Muted by default** — burned-in captions / on-screen text are essential, or
  the ad is meaningless to most viewers.

## Length sweet spots
- **TikTok:** 9–21s tends to perform; can run longer if genuinely engaging.
- **Meta Reels/feed:** 15–30s is the common sweet spot for DTC; hook still has to
  land in the first 3s regardless of total length.
- Very long ads (45s+) need to earn every second — flag draggy length.

## Sound expectations (affects scoring)
- **TikTok:** assume sound-on. A silent ad here is a missed opportunity.
- **Meta feed:** assume muted. The ad MUST work with captions / on-screen text;
  don't reward audio-only storytelling.
